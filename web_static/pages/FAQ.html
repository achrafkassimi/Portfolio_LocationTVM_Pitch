Refer to transportation options where users can rent scooters, motorcycles, or bicycles for short-term use.These services are typically app-based.

App-based rental services allow users to download a specific app, create an account, and choose a vehicle they want to rent , such as a bike, scooter, or motorcycle. The app uses GPS to show available vehicles nearby, which are typically parked in public spaces. Once a vehicle is selected, users can unlock it through the app, begin their ride, and pay based on the time or distance traveled. After use, the vehicle can be parked in designated areas or at a convenient location for the next rider to access.
